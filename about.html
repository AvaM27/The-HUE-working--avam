About The HUE

The HUE is a colour-based tool that explores how colour combinations shape mood, style, and visual meaning. Instead of treating colour as fixed, the website focuses on how colours interact and how those relationships can shift the feeling of something, whether it is an outfit or a composition. Users can choose a base colour and explore palettes connected to it, each one drawn from the natural world, including insects, plants, and feathers.

By linking these colour combinations to mood descriptions and outfit examples, the tool brings colour out of abstraction and into something more real and wearable. It encourages a slower, more thoughtful approach to choosing colour, where attention is given to contrast, balance, and tone rather than just trends or instinct. The goal is to make colour feel more intentional while still leaving room for personal style.
